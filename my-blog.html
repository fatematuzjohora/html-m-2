Rangamati tour

author: Fatema tuz johora

Why is Rangamati famous? Rangamati is also famous for cashew nut, water melon, Bangla Banana, fresh fishes of Kaptai lake. The district is also known to all for having a hydraulic Power Plant at Kaptai and Terrestrial Earth Satellite at Betbunia, Kawkhli.What is the best time to visit Rangamati? During winter, there is less water in the waterfall, but it reclaims its beauty in the monsoon. Those who want to experience natural waterfall can visit the incredible Shuvolong waterfalls in Shuvolong union of Barkal Upazila of Rangamati district.
